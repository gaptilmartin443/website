Trézor.io/Start® | Starting Up Your Device | Trézor®

Trézór.io/Start is the official platform for initiating your Trézór wallet setup. This user-friendly portal provides everything you need to configure your wallet, including installation instructions, security tips, and additional resources to help you manage your assets securely.

Getting Started: Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these steps to set up your Trézór wallet securely via Trézór.io/Start:

Step 1: Visit Trézór.io/Start

Begin by opening your browser and navigating to Trézór.io/Start. Ensure you are using the official website to avoid any security risks. Look for the secure HTTPS indicator in your browser’s address bar.

Step 2: Connect Your Device

  • Unbox your Trézór device and connect it to your computer or mobile device using the provided USB cable.

  • Ensure your device is powered on and properly connected before proceeding.

Step 3: Install the Trézór Suite

  • Download the Trézór Suite software from the official website.

  • Follow the installation prompts for your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux).

  • Once installed, open the Trézór Suite to continue the setup process.

Step 4: Create Your Wallet

  • Select the “Create Wallet” option within the Trézór Suite.

  • Follow the on-screen instructions to initialize your wallet.

  • Make sure to back up your recovery seed phrase. Write it down on the provided recovery card and store it in a secure, offline location.

Step 5: Update Firmware

  • Check for any available firmware updates to ensure your device is running the latest version.

  • Follow the instructions to update your firmware safely and avoid disconnecting your device during the process.

Step 6: Add Cryptocurrencies to Your Wallet

  • Navigate to the “Accounts” tab within the Trézór Suite.

  • Select your preferred cryptocurrency and follow the prompts to receive funds into your wallet.

  • Use the provided address or QR code to transfer assets securely.

Tips for a Safe Setup Process

  • Use a Trusted Device: Always use a computer or mobile device free from malware or viruses.

  • Secure Your Recovery Phrase: Never share your recovery phrase with anyone. Keep it offline and in a secure location.

  • Verify URLs: Double-check that you are visiting the official Trézór.io/Start website.

  • Enable Security Features: Activate additional security measures such as PIN protection and passphrase functionality.
